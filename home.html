Danlagan is a barangay in the municipality of Lopez, in the province of Quezon. Its population as determined by the 2020 Census was 3,190. This represented 3.37% of the total population of Lopez. The household population of Danlagan in the 2015 Census was 3,273 broken down into 667 households or an average of 4.91 members per household. Danlagan is situated at approximately 13.8873, 122.2680, on the island of Luzon. The elevation at these coordinates is estimated at 6.1 meters or 20.0 feet above mean sea level.
